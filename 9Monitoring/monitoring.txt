Monitoring means keeping an eye on your systems, servers, apps and websites.
It helps you to know if everything is working fine or if there are any issues that need attention. Here are some common monitoring tools and commands:

* What is Grafana?
Graphana is a data visualization tool.
It shows your system's data in beautiful dashboards, graphs and charts.

* What is Prometheus?
Prometheus is a monitoring and alerting toolkit.
It collects and stores metrics from your systems and applications.
It allows you to query and analyze the data to find out what's going on.

Prometheus and Grafana work together to give you a complete monitoring solution.
Prometheus collects the data and Grafana visualizes it.
